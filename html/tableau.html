Here is some simulated sales information for a global superstore.

I generated these visuals using Tableau. It is an excellent software that makes visualizing and analyzing various kinds of data easy, fast, and simple. Check it out at tableau.com!

The above image shows profits in the United States. California, New York, and Washington are the most profitable states while Texas, Ohio, and Pennsylvania are the least profitable.



California is the most profitable state. Profits peaked in late 2013, and there has been a general trend towards more profit in California over the past two years.



In particular, accessories are the most successful products sold in California, as noted by the profit levels compared with other kinds of products.



Looking ahead at accessory sales in California: The company anticipates peak sales for 2015 to be in January, with monthly sales fluctuating much like previous years.
