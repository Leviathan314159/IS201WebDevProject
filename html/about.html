About Me

Values, Goals, and Hobbies

My parents taught me to work hard, do good, and look for the best in people. I try to put people first in my life, especially when it comes to family. I am committed to honesty, integrity, and accountability in all that I do. I believe that having and living by core values is essential to being successful, and vital to creating a life of purpose and meaning.

I decided that I want to become an actuary because it makes good money, is helpful to people and the society, and has good job security. I also think it matches my strengths and skills, and the profession interests me!

I enjoy playing ultimate frisbee and soccer, and just about any board game. I also enjoy hiking with friends, cooking, and building things. When I have a spare minute you can often find me learning about nature and science, reading a book, searching for new recipes online, or thinking deeply in a quiet place. My idea of a perfect day would be to go for a morning run, spend the day with family and friends, and make Thai or Indian food for dinnertime.
